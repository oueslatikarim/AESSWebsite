IEEE AESS TUNISIA CHAPTER

Field of Interest

The scope of interest encompasses the organization, systems engineering, design, development, integration, and operation of intricate systems within space, air, ocean, or ground environments. This broad field encompasses a diverse range of systems, including but not limited to navigation, avionics, mobile electric power and electronics, radar, sonar, telemetry, military applications, law-enforcement systems, automatic test systems, simulators, and command and control structures. This comprehensive perspective reflects the multifaceted nature of our focus, highlighting the intricate interplay between various technological domains aimed at advancing and optimizing complex systems across different environments

Mission

The mission of AESS is to establish a dynamic and pertinent professional society that draws in, involves, supports, and preserves a diverse community of members worldwide. This diversity encompasses various dimensions, such as age, culture, and community perspectives—spanning theoretical, managerial, and practical applications—aligned with the fields of interest outlined in our constitution. AESS is committed to achieving this mission through a range of activities, including technical initiatives, chapter and society engagements, conferences, publications, education, technical operations, industry relations, and member services. Through these endeavors, AESS aims to create an inclusive and vibrant community that actively contributes to the advancement of knowledge and excellence in our shared fields.

Vision

The vision of AESS is to establish itself as an indispensable entity within the global technical community, renowned for its exceptional contributions in the aerospace and electronic systems fields. This recognition is to be substantiated through the society's impactful products, services, and offerings. AESS aspires to be a leader in various domains, including conferences, publications, education, technical operations, industry relations, and member services. Through these channels, AESS seeks to make enduring contributions and be acknowledged as a key player dedicated to advancing knowledge, fostering innovation, and providing valuable resources to its members and the broader technical community.
