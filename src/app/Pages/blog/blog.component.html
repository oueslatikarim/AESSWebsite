IEEE AESS TUNISIA CHAPTER TSYP TECHNICAL CHALLENGE

The primary goal of our challenge is to foster innovation and inspire participants to design advanced features for human-powered rovers. Through this challenge, we aim to encourage problem-solving skills, hands-on learning, and teamwork in the field of aerospace engineering, all while addressing real-world issues related to space exploration. Student Branches will participate with oneteam. Each team can have a maximum oftwo members who will present their ideasduring a 7-minute pitch. The winning Student Branch or Team will receive a prestigious award of 150 USD. The pitching phase is scheduled to takeplace at the IEEE TSYP 11, December 19th, 2023.

  • Medina Congress Center, Yasmine Hammamet Hotel Diar Lemdina Hammamet, Nabeul Tunisia

JOTA-JOTI

JOTA-JOTI 23 is the world's largest digital Scout event taking place in Tunisia. This educational event unites over 90 Scouts annually in October for a weekend of Scouting and fostering friendships. It offers young people the opportunity to explore communication technology and establish connections with fellow Scouts. We actively participated in this event to enhance the visibility of our chapter and its initiatives.
